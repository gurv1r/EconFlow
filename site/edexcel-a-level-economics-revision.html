Revising the Edexcel Economics Specification

The Edexcel specification is notoriously synoptic. You cannot just learn the themes in isolation. EconFlow helps you connect microeconomic theory to macroeconomic outcomes.

Theme breakdown

  • Theme 1 Economics revision: Introduction to markets and market failure. Focus on basic economic problem, price mechanism, and government intervention.
  • Theme 2 Economics revision: The UK economy – performance and policies. Focus on measures of economic performance, AD/AS, and macroeconomic policies.
  • Theme 3 Economics revision: Business behaviour and the labour market. Focus on market structures (perfect competition to monopoly) and the labour market.
  • Theme 4 Economics revision: A global perspective. Focus on international economics, poverty, inequality, and the financial sector.
