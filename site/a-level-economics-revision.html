How to Revise A-Level Economics

Success in A-Level Economics comes down to three main pillars: understanding the theory, evaluating with real-world examples, and practicing exam technique. You can't just memorise the textbook; you need to apply economic models to unseen scenarios.

Core Topics

  • Microeconomics Revision: Focus on supply and demand, market failure, elasticity, and business economics (costs, revenues, and market structures like monopoly and oligopoly).
  • Macroeconomics Revision: Focus on macroeconomic indicators (inflation, unemployment, economic growth), aggregate demand and supply (AD/AS), and global economics (international trade, exchange rates).
